**Purpose**: To ground complex terms in familiar concepts for a non-technical audience.

| Term                | Everyday Analogy           | Why This Matters                                                                               |

## 3. The Co-evolution Loop (The Network Intelligence)

**Purpose**: To visualize how SMBs get "Big Tech" R&D for $1.00.

- **Step 1: Local Optimism**: Agent A in Spoke 1 finds a faster way to optimize Lambda cold starts.
- **Step 2: Harvesting**: The Harvester abstracts this _pattern_ (with strict IP safety).
- **Step 3: Global Promotion**: The pattern is vetted and merged into the Hub.
- **Step 4: The Broadcast**: Every other Spoke on ClawMore receives the optimization via a `make sync`.
- **Outcome**: The entire Eclawnomy evolves simultaneously.
